About MGZ Properties

Run by alumni who

MGZ Properties is a boutique student housing company owned and operated by Binghamton University alumni. Having been students ourselves, we understand what renters value most: responsive communication, well-maintained homes, and a hassle-free living experience.

Our properties are centrally located, thoughtfully renovated, and fully furnished to help students feel at home from day one. We take pride in providing attentive service and treating our tenants with the same level of care and respect we would expect ourselves.
