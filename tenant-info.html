Winter Alternate Street Parking

From December 1st through March 15th, street parking in Binghamton follows alternate-side parking rules. The easiest way to remember it is: "Park for tomorrow."

How It Works

Each day at 5:00 PM, vehicles must be moved to the side of the street that matches the next day's date:

  • If tomorrow is an even-numbered day, park on the even side of the street.
  • If tomorrow is an odd-numbered day, park on the odd side of the street.

Example: On December 2nd (an even day), you must move your vehicle to the odd side of the street by 5:00 PM, because December 3rd is an odd day.

Garbage & Recycling

Garbage is collected every Wednesday morning. All trash must be placed in official City of Binghamton Blue Bags, which can be purchased at most grocery and convenience stores.

To comply with city regulations and help keep the neighborhood clean, please do not place garbage at the curb until after 6:00 PM on Tuesday evening.

Recycling should be placed in the provided City of Binghamton yellow recycling bin and may be set out at the same time as your garbage for collection.

You can get more information about trash pickup, or request a new recycling bin, by visiting the City of Binghamton Garbage Info page.

Utilities

Internet & Cable

Internet and cable service can be arranged directly through Spectrum. They can help you select a plan and provide any necessary equipment for your household.

Gas & Electric

If your lease requires tenants to pay utilities, gas and electric service must be established with NYSEG for the duration of the lease.

Service should be scheduled to initiate on the start date of your lease. To start service, contact NYSEG at (800) 572-1111 and provide your property address.

Water

Water service remains in the landlord's name. Water bills will be allocated to tenants and added as a separate utility charge through RentRedi.
